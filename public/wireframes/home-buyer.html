FAQ
How does Early Bird work?

Dealers post photos and prices the night before a flea market. You browse from your couch, save the pieces you want, and reach out to dealers directly — before the crowd shows up at 4am.

What's a "drop"?

Every market has a drop time, usually the evening before the market itself. At drop time, every dealer's booth goes live at once. Until then, the market is locked.

How do I contact a dealer?

Tap "I'm Interested" on any item. Write a short message. The dealer gets a text with your name, phone number, and message. From there you talk directly — call, text, or meet at the booth.

How do I pay?

You pay the dealer in person at the booth — cash, Venmo, Zelle, Apple Pay, or card, whichever the dealer accepts. Early Bird never handles payment.
